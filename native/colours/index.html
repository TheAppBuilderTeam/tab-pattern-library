The colours below are used throughout the mobile apps and each specifies the hexcode and the suggested name for each.

Text Colours

Below are the colours used on the text inside the app including headings, paragraphs, blockquotes, form inputs and placeholder text.

The colour PrimaryBodyColour is used for main body text used in the app including headings, paragraphs and form input text. Additionally forms will use SecondaryBodyColour for labels and placeholder text in the input field should be PlaceholderColour.

MetaTextColour is used in many instances in the app. For example, it is used for captions underneath images or video, social statistics, timestamps and helper text displayed under form fields.

  • PrimaryBodyColour

    #343D4C

  • SecondaryBodyColour

    #637289

  • PlaceholderColour

    #9CA9BC

  • MetaTextColour

    #738BB2

Border Colours

The colours below represent those used for borders in the app. BorderGrey is used commonly in form inputs and secondary button borders. SubtleGrey is used for subtle horizontal rules in the UI, for example in modular pages it is used for the lines around a blockquote.

  • BorderGrey

    #C9C9C9

  • SubtleGrey

    #E2E2E2

Links and Interactions

While the colour of many of the buttons inside the native apps are determined by the app branding (set by the App Editor), other links and the social buttons use the colour palette below. Secondary links usually found underneath Primary Buttons are blue, shown below as LinkColour.

A user can tap on the red heart to "like" content within the app, and tap the comment icon to post a comment. The colours used for these social icons are shown below.

  • LikeIconColour

    #D22B36

  • CommentIconColour

    #106DE0

  • LinkColour

    #738BB2

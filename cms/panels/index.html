Below are the content panels used throughout the CMS, they are displayed down each side of the CMS and are used for publishing, displaying tooltips & navigation

Publish Panel

The publish panel is where app editors can save and publish or schedule the publishing of their changes.

The publish panel features 3 buttons, Save, Save & Publish and schedule publish.

Buttons styling for the buttons within the publish panel can be found within the CMS Buttons page

Examples of the publish panel - Active state (left) Disabled state (right).

Schedule Publish

When a user first selects the schedule publish button, they will be presented with a calender and timer to choose when they want the post to be published. At this stage the confirm button is in the disabled state. When a user selected a date and time for publishing, the confirm button changes to the active state.
